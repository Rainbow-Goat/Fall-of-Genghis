# Fall-of-Genghis
Vic 2 mod about what would happen if Genghis Khan had a fear of heights.

I thought it would be fun to make a mod since I kept having stupid ideas for things you could do in a alt hist scenario so I made this.
The thought process behind every decision made in the making of this mod was "1. Is this interesting or funny? and 2. Can I somehow justify this happening?"
I dislike mods that just have random maps so I gave myself the (quite loose) restriction of having to argue for something happening due to the initial divergence.
The arguments aren't strong, but they do exist and imo that makes the mod more enjoyable as I prefer a semblance of thought behind the scenario.
If you want to make an MP version, or change the mod in anyway, shape or form that's cool, just make sure to credit me and whomever I have credited in my mod.
